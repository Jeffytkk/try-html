How to make a homemade pizza by yourself with simple ingredients

Pizza is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that you can enjoy at home. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients

For the crust: 1 cup flour, 1/2 cup warm water, 1 tsp yeast, 1/2 tsp salt

For the sauce: 1/2 cup tomato sauce, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp oregano

For the toppings: cheese (any kind), pepperoni or other preferred toppings

Instructions

  1. Prepare the crust by mixing flour, warm water, yeast, and salt. Knead until smooth and let rise for 10 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to fit your pizza pan.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the crust.
  5. Add your desired toppings.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

preparation time and cooking time

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 12-15 minutes
